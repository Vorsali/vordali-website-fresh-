What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored by a browser. Similar technologies may include local storage, pixels, and device identifiers.

How we use them

Vordali may use strictly necessary cookies to keep users signed in, maintain security, prevent fraud, preserve preferences, and support core site functions. We may also use limited analytics to understand performance and improve the service.

Third parties

Hosting, authentication, payment, analytics, and other service providers may set or read cookies according to their own policies.

Your choices

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Some Vordali features may not work correctly if necessary cookies are disabled.

Changes

We may update this policy as our use of technology changes.
