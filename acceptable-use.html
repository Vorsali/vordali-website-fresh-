Acceptable Use Policy

Activities that are prohibited when using Vordali.

Effective July 16, 2026

Purpose

This policy protects customers, businesses, carriers, payment networks, and Vordali from abuse.

Prohibited messaging

Users may not send unsolicited messages, purchased-list messages, deceptive content, unlawful marketing, phishing, harassment, or messages sent without required consent.

Prohibited transactions

Users may not request payment for illegal goods or services, fraudulent transactions, deceptive offers, unauthorized fundraising, or activity prohibited by Stripe or applicable law.

Platform abuse

Users may not attempt unauthorized access, interfere with service operation, introduce malicious code, scrape protected data, evade rate limits, or test vulnerabilities without written permission.

Identity and data

Users may not impersonate another person or business, submit false information, misuse personal data, or disclose credentials.

Enforcement

Vordali may investigate suspected violations and may suspend, restrict, or terminate access. We may cooperate with service providers and lawful authorities.

Reporting

Report suspected abuse to abuse@vordali.com or support@vordali.com.
